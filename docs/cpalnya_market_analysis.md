# CPALNYA Market Notes

Date: 2026-04-05

## Direct competitors

- `affLIFT`:
  strong community layer, follow-alongs, case studies, landing page examples, clear member value.
- `OfferVault`:
  powerful catalog/search model with structured metadata, filters, payout/geography clarity, offer-level utility.
- `CPALenta`:
  Russian-language taxonomy depth with tags, glossary, cases, news, interviews, services and vertical pages.

## Indirect competitors

- `AffMaven` / similar affiliate media:
  roundup/editorial model, community discovery, newsletter-style packaging of information.
- forum-style and news-style affiliate communities:
  recurring visits are driven by discussion, not only by static articles.

## UI conclusions used in CPALNYA

- Split the portal into editorial and utility layers instead of keeping everything inside one blog.
- Put ready-made assets into a dedicated section with article mode and download mode.
- Keep taxonomy visible and funnel-oriented:
  article -> related asset -> registration -> comments -> repeat visit.
- Use a strong dark editorial look with animated background to signal backstage/infrastructure/network themes.

## SEO cluster structure

- CPA / affiliate basics and glossary.
- Trackers, postbacks, BI, KPI, analytics.
- Team operations, reviews, workflows, scaling.
- Anti-detect, proxy, cloaking, infrastructure.
- Ready-made templates, kits, dashboards, checklists.

## Funnel model

- TOFU:
  glossary, explainers, comparisons, workflow articles.
- MOFU:
  ready-made assets, templates, implementation articles.
- BOFU:
  registration, comments, products, partner or service transitions.

## Implemented in this iteration

- new home page focused on CPA portal architecture;
- new `/solutions/` section with `downloads` and `articles` tracks;
- public users, auth forms, comment system, threaded rendering, formatting toolbar;
- view counters for article and solution detail pages;
- blog updated to surface CPALNYA-oriented content and community layer;
- host-specific blog seeding for initial CPA content;
- footer/nav adjusted to CPALNYA positioning.
